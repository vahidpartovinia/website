    • Story

    Vahid Partovi Nia was born and studied his B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Statistics in Mashhad.

    Mashhad's Location

    Mashhad is located in the north-eastern part of Iran and is the second biggest Iranian metropolitan after Tehran.

    A Touristic and A Holy City

    Mashhad hosts over million pilgrims and tourists per year.



    After completing his bachelor degree, he obtained top ranks in three different fields of statistics independently, namely Mathematical Statistics, Biostatistics, Actuarial and Social Statistics. He achieved these ranks in the national competition among almost 2000 B.Sc. graduates of statistics and was exempted from the two-year national military service as a reward for this extraordinary scientific achievement. Vahid continued his studies with a masters degree in mathematical statistics to provide a good theoretical background, but his wish to develop methods in the area of applied statistics remained alive. He is interested in methodological, application and computational aspects of statistics.


    He successfully held some part-time positions as university statistical consultant, experimental designer-analyst in the automobile industry and urban data analyst in municipality before starting his Ph.D. He won the Swiss Natinal Science Foundation's prospective researchers fund to develop his research ideas at McGill Unviersity and Stanford University, and then joined the department of Mathematics and Industrial Engineering of Ecole Polytechnique de Montreal.
