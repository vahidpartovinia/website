Vahid Partovi Nia

is Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics and Industrial Engineering of Ecole Polytechnique de Montreal. His main research interests are Data Science, Efficient Computing, Biostatistics, and Artificial Intelligence. He was Swiss SNF funded postdoctoral fellow at McGill University, visiting scholar at Stanford University, and received his Ph.D. in Statistics from Ecole Polytechnique Federale de Lasuanne, Switzerland under the supervision of Anthony Davison.
His hobbies include science, smart technology, data analysis, music, dance, computing, blogging, and Esperanto.
