Vahid Partovi Nia

is Principal Machine Learning Scientist at Noah's Ark Research Lab of Huawei Technologies, Adjunct Professor at Ecole Polytechnique de Montreal, and part time faculty at McGill University. He is a member of The Canada Excellence Research Chair in Data Science. His mission is to make a bridge between the expertise in academia, and the cutting-edge technology in the domain of machine learning and artificial intelligence in industry. His research interest is diverse and is application-oriented including Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Efficient Computing, and Biostatistics. He was Swiss SNF funded postdoctoral fellow at McGill University, visiting scholar at Stanford University, and received his Ph.D. in Statistics from Ecole Polytechnique Federale de Lasuanne, Switzerland.
His hobbies include science, smart technology, data analysis, music, dance, computing, blogging, and Esperanto.

Data Science

If you are professional looking for training in Machine Learning, Continuing Studies Certificate in Machine Learning and Data Science of McGill. If you are a graduate student, you may want to check MTH6312, a course on Statistical Machine Learning. I am writing a textbook on the subject with emphasis on concepts, application, and computation, check teaching page for more details.
Every semester I try to run a one-day hands-on Python Workshop and an R Workshop fovusing on Data Science and Artificial Intelligence at GERAD.

Keep your eye on Data Science program of University of British Columbia which is excellent one-year program in master's level.
